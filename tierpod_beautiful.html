How to Use TierPOD Pro

1. Upload Templates

Drag and drop or click to upload your mockup template images (t-shirts, mugs, etc.)

2. Position Elements

Select a mockup, click "Add Element", then drag/resize/rotate to position where your design should appear

3. Save Positions

Click "Save Position" to remember element settings. Repeat for all mockups.

4. Generate

Click "Generate Mockups" and select your design PNG files. The system will create all combinations automatically!
